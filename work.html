Flexible Approach

You can hire the whole team of trained developers, designers and managers. In such case, you get a considerable cost reduction and the advantages of all the team working in one office. Our managers control the workflow onsite.

OR

If you have a small project, which does not require the services of all our staff members, you may choose to hire one or several experienced developers for your dedicated team on the hourly, weekly or monthly basis

1Estimation and Planning

A starting point of your project is the initial idea. We encourage you to share your thoughts, notes and stories with us to understand your vision and desired outcome. Then we interview you for your business goals and collect requirements. During the negotiation process we discuss your preliminary budget and timeline. As a result we create User stories and Wireframes for your application. Sometimes it is necessary to divide the project into several Milestones.
